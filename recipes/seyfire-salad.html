Seyfire Salad

Ingredients:

  • 3 handfuls of spinach
  • 1 or 2 leaves lettuce
  • 1 roma tomato
  • 1/8 cup raisins
  • 1/2 small red onion
  • 1/8 cup candied walnuts
  • 1/4 cup dressing of your choosing

Steps:

  1. chop the lettuce
  2. add lettuce and spinach to salad spinner
  3. spin it boi
  4. add the rest of the ingredients
  5. toss the salad with your favorite dressing
  6. eat it

Description:

Easy Salad. Easy Life.
